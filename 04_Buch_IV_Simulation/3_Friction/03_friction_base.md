# 03_FRICTION: BASE LAYER
**Context:** Conflict, Crime, and Mental Health

## I. THE RESTITUTION PROTOCOL (Justice)
**Principle:** Damage creates Debt.
* **Mechanism:** Upon verification of harm, the aggressor's Resonance Account is debited.

## II. THE SOFT BLOCK (Conflict Resolution)
**Principle:** Separation > Litigation.
* **Algorithm:** If two Nodes generate repeated friction, the System executes a 'Soft Block' (moving them apart).

## III. THE BIO-RESTORE PROTOCOL
**Principle:** A breakdown is a maintenance event, not a moral failure.
