**Context:** The Developmental Phase (0-18 Years)
## I. THE WARD PROTOCOL (Status Definition)
A biological node between age 0 and 18 is classified as a **'Ward of the System'**.
* **Sovereignty:** The Ward is a sovereign entity, not the property of the parents.
* **Resource Guarantee:** The System automatically allocates a 'Growth Budget' directly to the Ward's ID.
## II. THE NEXUS INTERFACE (Educational Logistics)
Education is not a place (School), but a **Service**.
* **Algorithm:** The Nexus AI analyzes the Ward's interaction patterns to detect 'Sparks of Interest'.
